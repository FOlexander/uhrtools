How it works

HR faces many challenges in their daily work, and employee retention is one of the most significant issues. However, many HR managers struggle to see the full picture of employee retention in their company and are unsure how to identify key drivers that impact employee lifetime. Survival analysis can be an effective tool to help address these issues, as it can be applied to any data that has a start and end time.

This method can help you identify the probability and duration of an employee's tenure until a specific event occurs, such as dismissal or promotion. Additionally, it can help you discover key drivers that impact time to a specific event, assess the quality of recruiting, and more.
